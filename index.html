ABOUT ME

Leandra Turner recieved her BA degree in Biology from Concordia Univeristy Texas in 2006. Thereafter, she began working for Ascension Health, formerly Seton Healthcare Network, in the Histology department. While there she earned her certification as a Histology Technician in 2009 and was able to perform various aspects of histology to include: routine histology, muscle enzyme histochemistry, electron microscopy, immunohistochemistry.

She currently works in a Mohs dermatology clinic in Austin,TX, and is simutanousely taking a web development bootcamp course. She will finish the Triology Educations Services' Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp, through the University of Texas at Austin, in June 2019. After graduating she plans to pursue a career in the coding field.

Leandra prefers to spend her free time involved with her dog Callie. She also enjoys trying her hand at various hobbies that include: baking, knitting, gardening, and flower arranging. She also has a passion for fashion and has been known to be a "personal stylist" for her friends and family.
